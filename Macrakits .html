Our vision

Our vision at Macra Kits is a world where individuals have a personal connection to the products they use, and where sustainability is at the forefront of every consumer's mind. We aim to be a leader in the movement towards conscious consumption, and to inspire others to make ethical and eco-friendly choices in all aspects of their lives. Through our Macrame Bag Making Kits, we hope to promote creativity, connection, and conscious living.

Our mission

At Macra Kits, we are dedicated to providing our customers with a sustainable and personalized alternative to mass-manufactured bags. We believe that by empowering individuals to create their own bags, we can foster a deeper connection between consumers and the products they use. Our mission is to make a positive impact on the environment, promote ethical labor practices, and inspire creativity in our customers.
